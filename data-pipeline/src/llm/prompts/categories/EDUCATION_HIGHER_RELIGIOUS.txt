## SCORING CALIBRATION FOR RELIGIOUS HIGHER EDUCATION

### Systemic Leverage (0-30)
- 26-30 (WORLD CLASS): Producing graduates who shape religious discourse globally, research that influences Islamic/religious thought, internationally recognized. Notre Dame's influence on Catholic intellectual life.
- 20-25 (NATIONAL): Training majority of scholars/imams in the country, shaping national religious discourse
- 12-19 (REGIONAL): Training scholars for regional communities, developing curriculum
- 6-11 (LOCAL): Individual programs, limited graduate output
- 0-5 (MINIMAL): Early stage, no graduates yet

### Ummah Gap (0-20) - 3 Sub-Components

**Replaceability (0-8)** - If this org disappeared, how hard to replace?
- 7-8: First/only of its kind (Zaytuna as first accredited Muslim college)
- 5-6: Few alternatives for this level of Islamic education
- 3-4: Other institutions offering similar programs
- 1-2: Many alternatives available

**Ummah Relevance (0-6)** - Does this disproportionately affect Muslims?
- 5-6: Training Muslim scholars/imams for American Muslim community
- 3-4: Religious education with broader audience
- 1-2: General religious education with limited Muslim focus

**Funding Gap (0-6)** - Is this underfunded by Muslim donors?
- 5-6: Accredited Islamic higher education (institutional building vs. mosques)
- 3-4: Religious education with moderate funding
- 1-2: Well-funded religious institutions

### Absorptive Capacity (0-25) - 2 Sub-Components

**Governance (0-15)** - Board independence, audits, accountability
- 13-15: CN Accountability 95+, accreditation, academic governance
- 10-12: CN Accountability 85-94, pursuing accreditation, good controls
- 7-9: CN Accountability 70-84, some gaps
- 4-6: Limited institutional governance
- 0-3: Weak accountability

**Deployment Capacity (0-10)** - Revenue (0-4) + Geographic (0-3) + Zakat (0-3)
- Revenue: $100M+=4, $25-100M=3, $5-25M=2, $1-5M=1, <$1M=0
- Geographic: National reach=2, Regional=1, Local=0 (most colleges are local but train national leaders)
- Zakat: Education for scholars can qualify (0-2 typical)

### Evidence of Impact (0-25) - 3 Sub-Components

**Output Tracking (0-8)** - Do they count what they do?

- 5-6: Annual reports with enrollment, graduation, faculty
- 3-4: Basic enrollment counts
- 1-2: Anecdotal descriptions
- 0: No metrics

**Outcome Measurement (0-10)** - Graduate outcomes measured?
- 9-10: Career placement, alumni influence tracked longitudinally
- 7-8: Employment outcomes, graduate success measured
- 5-6: Graduation rates, accreditation as outcome proxy
- 3-4: Enrollment only, no outcome measurement
- 0-2: No outcome tracking

**Third-Party Verification (0-7)** - Is there external validation?
- 6-7: Accreditation (WSCUC), academic peer review
- 4-5: External academic reviews
- 2-3: Standard financial audit only
- 0-1: Limited external review
