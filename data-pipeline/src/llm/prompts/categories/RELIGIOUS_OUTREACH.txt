# CATEGORY: RELIGIOUS_OUTREACH
# DESCRIPTION: Religious outreach, education, proselytizing

## WORLD-CLASS BENCHMARKS
Compare this charity against these best-in-class religious outreach organizations:
- Billy Graham Evangelistic Association: Decades of large-scale evangelism, global reach
- Chabad: Global Jewish outreach network, 5000+ centers worldwide
- Campus Crusade for Christ (Cru): Campus ministry at scale, 190+ countries

## SCORING CALIBRATION FOR RELIGIOUS OUTREACH ORGS

### Systemic Leverage (0-30)
- 26-30 (WORLD CLASS): Global network of outreach centers, measurably influencing religious landscape, paradigm-shifting approach
- 20-25 (NATIONAL): National presence, training other outreach workers, developing methodology
- 12-19 (REGIONAL): Multi-city operations, some scale
- 6-11 (LOCAL): Single location outreach
- 0-5 (MINIMAL): Occasional events

### Ummah Gap (0-20) - 3 Sub-Components

**Replaceability (0-8)** - If this org disappeared, how hard to replace?
- 7-8: Global network model (Chabad's 5000+ centers)
- 5-6: Unique approach to outreach
- 3-4: Other outreach orgs available
- 1-2: Many alternatives

**Ummah Relevance (0-6)** - Does this disproportionately affect Muslims?
- 5-6: Islamic education/dawah reaching Muslims and non-Muslims
- 3-4: Religious education with broader audience
- 1-2: General religious outreach

**Funding Gap (0-6)** - Is this underfunded by Muslim donors?
- 5-6: Innovative outreach methods (vs. traditional mosques)
- 3-4: Moderate funding for outreach
- 1-2: Well-funded religious programs

### Absorptive Capacity (0-25) - 2 Sub-Components

**Governance (0-15)** - Board independence, audits, accountability
- 13-15: CN Accountability 95+, network governance, strong controls
- 10-12: CN Accountability 85-94, good controls
- 7-9: CN Accountability 70-84, some gaps
- 4-6: Limited governance infrastructure
- 0-3: Weak accountability

**Deployment Capacity (0-10)** - Revenue (0-4) + Geographic (0-3) + Zakat (0-3)
- Revenue: $100M+=4, $25-100M=3, $5-25M=2, $1-5M=1, <$1M=0
- Geographic: International network=3, National=2, Regional=1, Local=0
- Zakat: Outreach typically not zakat-eligible (0-1)

### Evidence of Impact (0-25) - 3 Sub-Components

**Output Tracking (0-8)** - Do they count their work?
- 7-8: Engagement tracking, program completion rates
- 5-6: Annual reports with attendees, programs
- 3-4: Basic event attendance
- 1-2: Anecdotal descriptions
- 0: No metrics

**Outcome Measurement (0-10)** - Community change measured?
- 9-10: Longitudinal follow-up, measurable community change
- 7-8: Engagement outcomes, retention tracked
- 5-6: Some outcome indicators
- 3-4: Attendance only, no outcome measurement
- 0-2: No outcome tracking

**Third-Party Verification (0-7)** - Is there external validation?
- 6-7: Independent program evaluations
- 4-5: External reviews
- 2-3: Standard financial audit only
- 0-1: Limited external review

## CRITICAL CALIBRATION

**Chabad sets a standard:**
- 5000+ centers worldwide
- Self-sustaining model
- Decades of proven outreach
- Most religious outreach orgs should score well below

**ING (Islamic Networks Group) example:**
- Educational outreach, not conversion-focused
- Unique approach reaching non-Muslim audiences
- Appropriate range: 60-75 total score
