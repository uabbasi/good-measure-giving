# Form 990 Structure Reference

Detailed breakdown of all 12 parts of IRS Form 990.

## Part I: Summary (Page 1)

The "snapshot" of the organization. Key lines:

| Line | Field | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Mission | Brief mission statement (limited to ~30 words on form) |
| 3 | Voting members | Number of voting board members |
| 4 | Independent members | Board members without conflicts of interest |
| 5 | Employees | Total number of employees |
| 6 | Volunteers | Estimated volunteer count |
| 8 | Contributions | Total contributions and grants received |
| 9 | Program revenue | Revenue from program services |
| 10 | Investment income | Dividends, interest, capital gains |
| 12 | **Total revenue** | Sum of all revenue sources |
| 13 | Grants paid | Grants and similar amounts paid out |
| 17 | Other expenses | All other functional expenses |
| 18 | **Total expenses** | Sum of all expense categories |
| 19 | Revenue less expenses | Net income/loss for year |
| 20 | Total assets (BOY) | Beginning of year assets |
| 21 | Total assets (EOY) | End of year assets |
| 22 | Total liabilities (EOY) | End of year liabilities |
| 22 | Net assets (EOY) | Assets minus liabilities |

## Part II: Signature Block

Administrative information:

- Name and title of officer signing
- Date signed
- Preparer's name, firm, address
- Preparer's PTIN (Preparer Tax ID)
- Self-prepared checkbox

**Useful for**: Determining filing timeliness, identifying accounting firm

## Part III: Statement of Program Service Accomplishments

| Line | Field | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Mission statement | Organization's exempt purpose (expanded) |
| 2 | New programs | Description of significant new activities |
| 3 | Ceased programs | Programs discontinued during year |
| 4a | Program 1 | Description, achievements, beneficiaries, expenses |
| 4b | Program 2 | Second largest program by expenses |
| 4c | Program 3 | Third largest program by expenses |
| 4d | Other programs | Additional programs (list on Schedule O) |
| 4e | **Total program expenses** | Sum of all program service expenses |

**Key insight**: Compare program expenses to Part IX totals for consistency.

## Part VII: Compensation

### Section A: Officers, Directors, Trustees, Key Employees

Columns for each listed person:

| Column | Data |
|--------|------|
| (A) | Name and title |
| (B) | Average hours/week |
| (C) | Position checkboxes (officer, director, etc.) |
| (D) | Reportable compensation from org |
| (E) | Reportable compensation from related orgs |
| (F) | Other compensation (benefits, deferred) |

**Thresholds**:
- List anyone receiving >$100k from org + related orgs
- List all officers regardless of compensation
- List 5 highest compensated employees >$100k

### Section B: Independent Contractors

| Threshold | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| >$100k | Must list name, services, compensation |
