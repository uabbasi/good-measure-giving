07Correcting or updating our data

A defined process, not an inbox black hole
  1. Tell us what's wrong — via goodmeasuregiving.org, with the data point, what it should be, and where the correct value is published.
  2. We triage into one of three lanes. We misread a source → we fix the pipeline and re-crawl. The data isn't public yet → you publish it (section 03 shows where we read each field), then we re-run. You dispute the methodology → logged in our public issue tracker for the next rubric version.
  3. Your evaluation is re-run end-to-end — never hand-edited. Scores only ever come from the pipeline.
  4. Every change is version-controlled — we can show you exactly what changed and when.

Re-evaluations are typically completed within two weeks of a verified correction.

How to update what we see

Your website: publish your zakat policy, program outcomes, beneficiary counts, board composition, and annual reports where a crawler can find them. Candid: keep your profile current — Gold/Platinum seals feed the transparency signal. Charity Navigator: an up-to-date evaluation feeds verification. IRS Form 990: file on time — we read ratios, reserves, and governance from it. Then contact us and we'll re-run your evaluation.
